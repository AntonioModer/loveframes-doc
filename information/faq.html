Q: Can I use Love Frames in my project if I plan to go commercial with my project?
A: Yes

Q: Do I have to release my project under Love Frame's license if I use Love Frames in my project?
A: No. The only requirement of Love Frame's license is that you attribute the author in your work.

Q: How should I attribute the author in my work?
A: All I ask is that somewhere in your credits you state that your project uses Love Frames and that I am the author of Love Frames. I'd prefer if you used my real name which is Kenny Shields, but I won't mind if you use Nikolai Resokav either.
